# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** June 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy governs the **Rule Glyph Lab** mobile application and explains how we manage your data.

## 1. Information We Collect
The data processed depends on your interaction with specific features of the app. Because Rule Glyph Lab operates locally on your device, we process data without transferring it to external servers.

### 1.1 Local Game Progress and Settings
We store your level progression, unlocked chapters, and audio/control preferences locally on your device.

### 1.2 Local Network Multiplayer
For local network multiplayer, the app uses your local area network (LAN) connection solely to establish host-client communication with nearby devices. No connection details, IP addresses, or multiplayer communications are stored or transmitted outside your local network.

> **Note:** All level progression, settings, and local network multiplayer session handshakes are stored/processed strictly locally on your device.

## 2. How We Use Information
We utilize the collected data to:
* Manage and persist your game level progression and options.
* Enable local network multiplayer connection setup.

## 3. When Data Leaves Your Device
Rule Glyph Lab is a local-only application. Your personal data and gameplay progress do not leave your device.

## 4. Sharing and Service Providers
We do not share your personal information. All gameplay records and settings are kept strictly local.

## 5. Data Retention
* **Local History:** All records are retained locally on your device until you choose to reset your progress inside the app settings, delete the application data in system settings, or uninstall the app.

## 6. Your Choices and Rights
You have the following controls:
* **Local Data Management:** Access, review, and reset your level progress directly from the Options dialog inside the app.
* **App Data Reset:** You can delete all local data by uninstalling the application or using your device settings to clear the app's cache and data.

## 7. Security and Children's Privacy
* **Security:** Since your data is stored locally within the application's secure storage, we recommend securing your device with a passcode, PIN, or biometric lock.
* **Children:** Rule Glyph Lab is a logical puzzle game and safe for children of all ages. No personal information is collected by us.

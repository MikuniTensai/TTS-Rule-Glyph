## 3. Data Deletion Instructions
Since Rule Glyph Lab works completely offline and stores your data solely on your device, you have total ownership and control over your data deletion.
* **How to delete your local history records:** Go to options inside the app and select "Reset Progress", which removes all unlocked levels.
* **How to completely remove all data:** Uninstall the application from your device, or clear the application cache/data through your device's app settings.

## 4. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### Q: Where is my data stored?
A: Your level progression and settings are stored entirely offline on your device's internal storage using shared preferences. They are never sent to external servers.

### Q: Why is local multiplayer not connecting?
A: Ensure both devices are connected to the same local area network (LAN), that they can communicate with each other, and that local network permissions are granted in system settings.

### Q: How do I change control layouts?
A: Under the Options menu, you can customize the Dpad size (Kecil/Sedang/Besar) and location (Kiri/Kanan) to suit your preferences.
